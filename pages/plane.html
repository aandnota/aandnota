Plane Positions Array

Databent 3D models

In these images, I've taken transcripts from 90 minutes of pager intercepts in NYC on 9-11-200, transcoded them into vector coordinates, and injected these coordinates into a found bust. All of the images show the injection in the "plane positions array" section of the .DAE format. Coupled with a glitch in the transcoding where I placed a "-" before every 0 which would sometimes result in impossible coordinates like -0.3325-03.
